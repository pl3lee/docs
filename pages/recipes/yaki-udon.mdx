# Yaki Udon
Taken from [this](https://drivemehungry.com/yaki-udon-stir-fried-udon-noodles/) site.

## Ingredients
- 2 packs of udon noodles
- Vegetables
- Green onions
- Protein
- 2.5 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp rice wine vineger

## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
Mix 2.5 tbsp dark soy sauce, 2 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tbsp mirin, 2 tsp sugar, and 1/2 tsp rice wine vinegar in a small bowl and set aside.
### Step 2
Cook udon noodles until separated (approx 2 mins). Don't cook for too long.
### Step 3
Drain udon noodles and rinse with cold water
### Step 4
In a large pan, heat oil and stir fry meat until almost cooked.
### Step 5
Add in vegetables and stir fry until soft
### Step 6
Add in udon noodles, sauce, and green onions. Stry fry until the sauce is reduced enough and the noodles are heated through.
</Steps>
