# Soy Maple Glazed Salmon
## Ingredients
- Salmon fillet
- 2.5 tbsp real maple syrup
- 1.5 Tbsp soy sauce (not low-sodium)
- 1 garlic clove
- 0.5 tsp salt, pepper, garlic powder

## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
In a mixing bowl whisk together maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, and seasoning.
### Step 2
Place salmon into a baking dish then pour maple syrup mixture evenly over salmon.
### Step 3
Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes, carefully rotating salmon once halfway through. Preheat oven to 400 degrees during last 10 minutes of marinating salmon.
### Step 4
Spray a 13 by 9 inch baking dish with cooking spray then transfer marinated salmon into greased dish (don't discard marinade). Bake in preheated oven until salmon is cooked through, about 15 minutes.
### Step 5
Meanwhile, pour marinade into a small saucepan, bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring frequently. 
### Step 6
Once it reaches a boil, reduce heat slightly and allow to cook several minutes, stirring frequently, until reduced to 1/4 cup.
### Step 7
Serve salmon warm with 1 Tbsp of the maple soy glaze drizzled over top and black pepper and green onions if desired

</Steps>
