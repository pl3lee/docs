# Ground Beef Egg with Rice
Recipe taken from [this](https://youtu.be/WkntFgDZPQI?si=7jrYVbJKkndnfemg) video.
## Ingredients
- 0.25 onion
- 50ml dashi
- 1 tbsp sake
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 egg
- ground beef/pork
- green onions
- rice
## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
Cut onion into thin slices.
### Step 2
Chop some green onions.
### Step 3
Make the sauce by mixing 50ml dashi, 1 tbsp sake, 1 tbsp mirin, 1 tbsp soy sauce, and 1 tsp sugar.
### Step 4
Crack an egg into a bowl and beat it.
### Step 5
Add oil to pan, stir fry onion until translucent.
### Step 6
Add ground meat to pan, no need to stir fry too much. Instead pan fry it a bit until the bottom is browned, then start to stir fry.
### Step 7
Add the sauce to the pan, wait until it starts to boil.
### Step 8
Add the beaten egg to the pan, cook until the egg is reaches your desired consistency.
### Step 9
Slide the egg and meat over a bowl of rice, garnish with green onions.
</Steps>
