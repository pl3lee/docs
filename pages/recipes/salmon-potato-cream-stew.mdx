# Salmon Potato Cream Stew
Recipe taken from [this](https://youtu.be/jFKqhfTwSw4?si=6r5iN00HaAX1Fx-Q) video.
## Ingredients
- Salmon
- 1 - 1.5 Potatoes
- 1/4 Onion
- 1/4 Carrot
- 300ml dashi
- 2tbsp sake
- 1 tbsp mirin
- Around 2 tbsp miso
- 100ml milk
- 15g butter

## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
Cut salmon into bite-sized pieces, don't cut too small
![](/images/recipes/salmon-potato-cream-stew/salmon.png)
### Step 2
Marinate salmon with 2tbsp sake, some salt and pepper for 10 minutes
### Step 3
Peel and cut potatoes and carrots into bite-sized pieces. Soak the potatoes in water.
### Step 4
Cut onions into small slices
### Step 5
Prepare large pan, add some oil and stir fry onions until they become translucent
### Step 6
Add in potatoes and carrots and stir fry for a few minutes
### Step 7
Add in 300ml dashi, 100ml milk, 1tbsp mirin
### Step 8
Put 2tbsp miso in a small bowl, add some soup from the pan and mix well. Add half of the miso mixture to the pan. Boil until potatoes are cooked
### Step 9
Add in the remaining miso mixture and 15g butter and mix well
### Step 10
Add in the marinated salmon. To prevent the salmon from falling apart, do not mix it too much, just pour it in and let it cook for around 3 minutes (or until the salmon is cooked)
</Steps>
