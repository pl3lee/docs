# Teriyaki Chicken
## Ingredients
- Chicken Thighs
- Teriyaki Sauce: 1:2:1:2, japanese soy sauce, mirin, japanese cooking sake, sugar
- Marinade: Salt, soy sauce, pepper, garlic powder (can be whatever)

## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
If using chicken thighs, make shallow slices in the meat, just deep enough to sever the tendons without fully cutting through the meat.
### Step 2
Marinate thighs for 30 mins (Salt, soy sauce, pepper, garlic powder (can be whatever)).
### Step 3
Make teriyaki sauce with ratio 1:2:1:2 for Japanese soy sauce, mirin, Japanese cooking sake, sugar.
### Step 4 (Optional)
After marinating, coat thighs in a layer of flour or corn starch (optional).
### Step 5
Heat cooking oil in a pan, pan fry chicken thighs until lightly browned and crisp.
### Step 6
In another pot, pour in teriyaki sauce, boil it for 1-2 mins.
### Step 7
Wipe off oil from pan that was used for pan frying the thighs (or could just transfer the thighs to a different pan, no oil)
### Step 8
Pour in the boiled teriyaki sauce and allow to cook, while stirring, until the sauce thickens into a beautiful shiny glaze (about 2-3 minutes).
</Steps>
