# Yaki Udon with Pork
Recipe taken from [this](https://youtu.be/8owBsNa_m5g?si=RpG3h7B05332N6wR) video.
## Ingredients (2 servings)
- 2 Udon
- Sliced pork (can replace with chicken)
- 1/4 Onions
- Carrots, around the same amount as onion
- 1/4 Cabbage
- Suitable amounts of green onions
- 80ml dashi
- 3 tbsp sake
- 1.5 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- salt and pepper
## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
Cut carrots into thin strips
![](/images/recipes/yaki-udon-with-pork/carrots.png)
### Step 2
Cut cabbage into bite-sized pieces
![](/images/recipes/yaki-udon-with-pork/cabbages.png)
### Step 3
Cut onions into thin slices
![](/images/recipes/yaki-udon-with-pork/onions.png)
### Step 4
Cut green onions into small pieces
### Step 5
Cut pork into bite-sized pieces
### Step 6
Mix 80ml dashi, 3tbsp sake, 1.5tbsp mirin, 2tbsp soy sauce in a bowl
### Step 7
Prepare a large pan, add oil and turn on the heat.
### Step 8
Pan fry the pork until slight brown, then add some salt and pepper. Turn off the heat and set aside.
### Step 9
Reuse the pan and stir fry onions and carrots together. After the onion is translucent, add cabbage and stir fry until it's soft.
### Step 10
Add in the udon (no need to boil) and pour in the sauce. Turn heat to medium-low, cover and cook for 2 minutes.
### Step 11
Flip the udon and cover and cook for another 2 minutes.
### Step 12
Turn to high heat, add in the pork and green onions. Stir fry until the sauce is reduced, and also add some salt and pepper to taste.
</Steps>
