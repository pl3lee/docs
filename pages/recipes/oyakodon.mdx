# Chicken and Egg with Rice (Oyakodon)
Recipe taken from [this](https://youtu.be/EiCNYPBZ6As?si=HAhKnwV1vtPIdmI9) video.
## Ingredients
- 2 Chicken Thighs
- 1/2 Onion
- 2 Eggs
- Green onions
- 4tbsp dashi
- 1tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin

## Instructions
<Steps>
### Step 1
Cut onions into small slices
### Step 2
Make shallow slices in the meat, just deep enough to sever the tendons without fully cutting through the meat.
### Step 3
Prepare the sauce by mixing 4tbsp dashi, 1tbsp soy sauce, and 1tbsp mirin. In general, just keep the ratio 4:1:1 for dashi, soy sauce, and mirin. You can adjust the amount of sauce based on the amount of chicken you have.
### Step 4
In a large pan, add sauce then the onions, keep the heat on medium and wait for it to boil. Once boiled, keep heat on low.
### Step 4
In another pan, pan fry the chicken thighs until both sides are browned
### Step 5
Once the chicken is browned, cut the chicken into slices and add it to the pan with the sauce and onions.
### Step 6
Beat 2 eggs and pour in half of the eggs over the chicken and onions. Cook on high heat for around 1 min.
### Step 7
Pour the rest of the eggs ov er the chicken and onions and cook for another 2-3 mins until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
</Steps>
